What do I like doing on my spare time?

Photography

Often I like taking pictures of NYC's horizon, but from time to time, I tend to take pictures of anything that looks appealing to me. Some times I convince myself that I can get a wickedly good angle, and the next I know I'm angling away. I like to try different angles as it gives my pictures life, and I also try to change up my style here and there. Be sure to check out my instagram, linked below, for some sample pictures.

Tinkering

I like to tinker with things from time to time, varying from Arduinios, to iPhones, or Computers. When I'm not familiar with a contraption, I tend to open them carefully, while sometimes making a corresponding blueprint as a future reference. However, most times it would be to repair the contraption, or to take parts from it. Fun Fact: The image below is actually a robotic arm that I made, mounted on a robotic car.

Skateboarding

I started skating during my first year of high school. At first, I was pretty worried with getting hurt, but that didn't stop me from wanting to accomplish my goal of mastering the skateboard. At first, I did it because I saw something more from skateboarding, as if it was a form of relaxtion. Not just a form of transportion, and that's the same now, but now I feel like my thoughts don't feel strained when I skate.
